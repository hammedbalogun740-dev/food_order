FAQ

Common questions about working with me.

What services do you offer?
I offer website development, UI/UX design, responsive design, wireframing & prototyping, and landing page design. My focus is on creating clean, user-friendly digital experiences.
How long does a typical project take?
It depends on the scope. A simple landing page can take 3–5 days, while a full multi-page website or UI design project can take 1–3 weeks. I always confirm timelines before starting.
Do you work with clients remotely?
Yes! I work fully remotely and have experience collaborating with clients online. I communicate clearly and keep clients updated throughout the entire process.
Are you open to internship or junior roles?
Absolutely. I'm actively looking for opportunities to grow, collaborate, and contribute to meaningful projects — whether as a freelancer, intern, or junior team member.
What tools and technologies do you use?
I primarily work with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Python. For design, I use Figma and design tools that help me prototype layouts and user interfaces before development.
